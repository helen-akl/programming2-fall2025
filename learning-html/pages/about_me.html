HELEN RAHAEL AKLILU

Helen Aklilu was born in Winston-Salem, North Carolina. The esteemed mime discovered her love for mimicry at the bottom of a McDonald's Ballpit. In between the neon plastic balls and piss a passion for theatre blossomed. She began performing at local grocery stores and eventually, after 2 days of hard work and practice, the White House.
